🔍 How to explore
Click anywhere on the image to zoom in 3× at that point. Use Zoom Out to zoom 3× out from the current center. Type coordinates into the center field (e.g. -0.745, 0.113) and press Zoom In — an orange rectangle will preview the target area. The most interesting detail is at the boundary between black (inside) and colour (outside)!
🎨 How the colour works
Each pixel is a number c on the complex plane. We iterate the formula and count steps to "escape." Smooth colouring (μ = n + 1 − log(log|z|)/log 2) maps step count to the selected palette. Points that never escape are black — they're inside the fractal.
